About Me

I am an amateur photographer based (for the moment) in Toulouse, France. I started with photography in 2020 and have been doing it since. I am passionate about photographing urban and suburban landscapes, nature, and life in the city. I mostly photograph in the cities I travel to. I am mostly interested in capturing those hidden spots of the cities, where I believe the true nature and and soul of them lies. I am seen mostly seeking the dark alleyways, always on the lookout for that interplay between the light and the shadows to which I am attracted to. Enjoy!
